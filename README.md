# ✝️ The Life of Jesus — An Interactive Ebook

An interactive, visual journey through the life of Jesus Christ, told across a series of beautifully animated levels. Each level is a self-contained HTML page featuring hand-crafted CSS illustrations, step-by-step storytelling, and scripture references — no images, no frameworks, just code brought to life.

---

## 📖 About

This project retells the story of Jesus from birth to resurrection through interactive browser-based scenes. Each level invites the reader to tap or click through key moments, watching the story unfold with animated characters, environmental effects, and scripture passages drawn directly from the Bible.

The visual style is warm and approachable — golden desert palettes, soft gradients, and gentle animations — designed to feel reverent yet accessible to readers of all ages.
